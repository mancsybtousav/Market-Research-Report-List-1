<p><p>Electroplating pretreatment agents can be categorized into three main types based on their pH levels: acidic, neutral, and alkalinity. Acidic pretreatment agents are used to clean and etch the metal surface before plating. Neutral pretreatment agents are used for intermediate cleaning processes, while alkaline pretreatment agents are used to remove any remaining contaminants and prepare the surface for plating. Each type of pretreatment agent serves a specific purpose in the electroplating process, ensuring the proper adhesion and quality of the plated surface.</p></p>
