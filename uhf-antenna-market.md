<p><p>UHF antennas come in two main types: Omnidirectional and Orientate. </p><p>Omnidirectional antennas are designed to receive signals from all directions, making them ideal for areas where signals can come from various angles. They are commonly used in urban areas where signals may be coming from multiple transmission towers.</p><p>On the other hand, Orientate antennas are directional and need to be pointed towards the transmitter to receive signals effectively. They are often used in rural areas where signals come from a specific direction.</p></p>
