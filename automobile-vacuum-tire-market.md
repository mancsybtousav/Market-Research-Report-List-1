<p><p>Automobile vacuum tire market can be divided into two main types: Radial Tires and Bias (Slash) Tires. Radial tires have cords that run at a 90-degree angle from the direction of travel, providing better traction, fuel efficiency, and longer tread life. On the other hand, Bias (Slash) tires have cords that run diagonally across the tire, providing better load-carrying capacity and stability. Both types have their own advantages and are suited for different driving conditions and vehicles.</p></p>
