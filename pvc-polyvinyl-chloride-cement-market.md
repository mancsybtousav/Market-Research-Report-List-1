There are two main types of PVC pipes in the market, based on their thickness: 40 PVC Pipe and 80 PVC Pipe. The 40 PVC Pipe is thinner and lighter, suitable for low-pressure applications and irrigation systems. 80 PVC Pipe, on the other hand, is thicker and stronger, designed for high-pressure systems such as industrial applications and water mains. Both types require the use of compatible PVC Cement for secure and leak-proof joints.</p></p>
