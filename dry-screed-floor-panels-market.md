<p><p>Dry screed floor panels are commonly available in three market types: gypsum fiber, mineral fiber, and polyurethane. Gypsum fiber panels are made from a combination of gypsum and cellulose fibers, providing good thermal and acoustic insulation properties. Mineral fiber panels are composed of various mineral fibers, offering high durability and fire resistance. Polyurethane panels are made of synthetic materials, providing excellent moisture resistance and thermal conductivity. Each market type caters to different needs and preferences in the construction industry.</p></p>
