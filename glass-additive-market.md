<p><p>The glass additive market can be categorized into four main types based on the materials used: Metal Oxides, Nanoparticles, Rare Earth Metals, and Others. Metal Oxides such as titanium dioxide and iron oxide are commonly used to enhance the properties of glass. Nanoparticles are tiny particles that can improve clarity, strength, and thermal resistance of glass. Rare Earth Metals like cerium and neodymium can enhance the optical properties of glass. Other types of additives include various materials like boron and fluorine which can provide specific functionalities to glass products.</p></p>
