<p><strong>The Single-Phase Stepper Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Variable-reluctance (VR)</li><li>Permanent Magnet (PM)</li><li>Hybrid (HB)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single-phase stepper motors are classified into three main market types based on their design: Variable-reluctance (VR), Permanent Magnet (PM), and Hybrid (HB). Variable-reluctance motors utilize a simple construction with a wound stator and a rotor with salient poles, making them cost-effective but less efficient. Permanent Magnet motors have a rotor with permanent magnets, providing higher efficiency and holding torque. Hybrid motors combine the best of both VR and PM designs, offering improved performance and accuracy for various applications.</p></p>
