<p><strong>The Rail Track Inspection Vehicle Market Industry Research by Application is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ballastless Track</li><li>Ballast Track</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rail track inspection vehicles are used in both ballastless track and ballast track applications to inspect the condition of tracks and ensure safe operation of trains. Ballastless track systems use concrete slabs instead of traditional ballast stones, requiring specialized inspection vehicles to assess the condition of the track structure. In ballast track systems, where tracks are supported by gravel or crushed rock, inspection vehicles are utilized to identify any issues with the track bed and ensure proper maintenance and repair.</p></p>
