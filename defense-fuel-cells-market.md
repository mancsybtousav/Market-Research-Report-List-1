<p><p>Defense Fuel Cells market consists of three main types: Proton Exchange Membrane Fuel Cells (PEMFC), Solid Oxide Fuel Cells (SOFC), and Direct Methanol Fuel Cells (DMFC). PEMFCs are lightweight and efficient, making them ideal for portable applications in defense systems. SOFCs are known for their high efficiency and low emissions, making them suitable for stationary power generation in military installations. DMFCs use methanol as a fuel, providing a high energy density and long runtime, making them suitable for applications requiring long endurance.</p></p>
