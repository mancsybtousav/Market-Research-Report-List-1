<p><p>Smart shoe market types include step counting shoes, positioning shoes, and navigation shoes. Positioning shoes utilize GPS technology to accurately track the wearer's location in real-time. Navigation shoes offer turn-by-turn directions through built-in technology, aiding users in easily finding their way around unfamiliar areas. These different types of smart shoes cater to varying needs, from fitness tracking to improving overall navigation and location-based services.</p></p>
