<p><p>Low-Volatile Organic Compounds (VOC) coating additives can be classified into various types based on their functions. These include rheology modifiers, which help control the flow and consistency of the coating. Defoamers are used to eliminate foam formation during application. Wetting agents improve the adhesion of the coating to the substrate. Dispersants help in dispersing solid particles evenly in the liquid coating. Other types of additives may include anti-settling agents, anti-blocking agents, anti-skinning agents, and UV stabilizers.</p></p>
