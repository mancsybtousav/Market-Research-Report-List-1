<p><strong>The Aluminum Die Casting Parts Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vacuum Die Casting</li><li>Pressure Die Casting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aluminum die casting parts market is divided into two main types: Vacuum Die Casting and Pressure Die Casting. Vacuum die casting involves removing air from the die cavity before injecting molten aluminum, resulting in high-quality, void-free parts. On the other hand, Pressure die casting involves injecting molten aluminum into the die cavity at high pressure to ensure fast and precise casting. Both methods are widely used in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and electronics for producing complex aluminum parts efficiently.</p></p>
