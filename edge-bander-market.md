<p><p>Edge banders are machines used in the woodworking industry to apply edge banding to the edges of various panels. The market for edge banders is divided into two main types: single-sided edge banders and double-sided edge banders. Single-sided edge banders are designed to only process one side of the panel at a time, while double-sided edge banders can simultaneously process both sides. Single-sided edge banders are typically more affordable and suitable for smaller woodworking operations, while double-sided edge banders are more efficient for larger production facilities.</p></p>
