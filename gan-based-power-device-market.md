<p><p>GaN (Gallium Nitride) is a wide bandgap semiconductor material that is used in the production of high-performance power devices. GaN based power devices offer several advantages over traditionally used Silicon-based power devices, such as higher breakdown voltage, faster switching speed, and lower on-resistance. These properties make GaN based power devices more efficient, compact, and cost-effective in applications including power electronics, renewable energy systems, electric vehicles, and consumer electronics.</p><p>The GaN Based Power Device Market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. The market growth can be attributed to the increasing adoption of GaN based power devices across various industries and the growing demand for high-performance power electronic devices. The rising need for power efficient devices, coupled with the increasing trend of miniaturization in electronics, is also driving the market growth.</p><p>Furthermore, the increasing deployment of GaN based power devices in electric vehicles, data centers, and the aerospace industry is expected to propel the market growth.
