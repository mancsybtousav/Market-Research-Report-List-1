<p><p>Type-1 Diabetes Treatment Market includes three main types - Rapid-Acting Insulin, Short-Acting Insulin, and Others. Rapid-Acting Insulin is absorbed quickly into the bloodstream, helping to control blood sugar levels after meals. Short-Acting Insulin takes effect within 30 minutes and peaks within 2-3 hours. Other treatments may include long-acting insulins, oral medications, and insulin pumps. These treatment options aim to mimic the body's natural insulin production and help regulate blood sugar levels in individuals with Type-1 Diabetes.</p></p>
