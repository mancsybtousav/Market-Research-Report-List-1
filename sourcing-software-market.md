<p><p>The sourcing software market consists of two types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise software is installed and operated on the company's own servers or computers, requiring maintenance and updates to be handled internally. On the other hand, cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet. It offers the advantage of being easily scalable, with updates and maintenance managed by the software provider. Both types have their pros and cons, so companies need to carefully consider their specific needs and infrastructure before choosing the most suitable option.</p></p>
