<p><ul><li>AC</li><li>DC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>AC and DC are two types of starter generators used in airplane systems. AC starter generators provide alternating current, which is commonly used in larger aircraft due to its efficiency and power capabilities. On the other hand, DC starter generators provide direct current and are often used in smaller aircraft due to their simplicity and reliability. Both types play a crucial role in starting and supplying power to the aircraft's electrical systems, but their suitability depends on the size and requirements of the aircraft.</p></p>
