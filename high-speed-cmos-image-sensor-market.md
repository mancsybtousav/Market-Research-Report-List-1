<p><strong>The High Speed CMOS Image Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Front-illuminated (FI) CMOS</li><li>Backside-illuminated (BSI) CMOS sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High Speed CMOS Image Sensor Market can be divided into two main types: Front-illuminated (FI) CMOS sensors and Backside-illuminated (BSI) CMOS sensors. </p><p>Front-illuminated sensors have the sensor array located at the front of the silicon substrate, which allows for easier manufacturing but may lead to lower sensitivity. On the other hand, backside-illuminated sensors have the sensor array located at the back of the substrate, allowing for higher sensitivity due to better light capture efficiency. Both types have their own advantages and are used in different applications based on their specific characteristics.</p></p>
