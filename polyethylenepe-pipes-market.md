<p><ul><li>HDPE(High-density polyethylene) Pipe</li><li>MDPE(Medium -density polyethylene) Pipe</li><li>LDPE(Low-density polyethylene) Pipe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyethylene pipes are divided into three main types based on their density - HDPE, MDPE, and LDPE. HDPE pipes are known for their high strength and durability, making them suitable for use in applications requiring high pressure and temperature resistance. MDPE pipes offer a balance between strength and flexibility, making them ideal for gas and water distribution. LDPE pipes are flexible and widely used for irrigation and agriculture. Each type of polyethylene pipe serves a specific purpose based on its density and properties.</p></p>
