<p><p>The commercial drone market can be classified into four types: fixed-wing, rotary blade, nano, and hybrid drones. Fixed-wing drones resemble traditional airplanes and are known for their long flight endurance and ability to cover extensive areas. Rotary blade drones, such as quadcopters, are popular for their stability, agility, and vertical take-off and landing capabilities. Nano drones are compact and lightweight, suitable for indoor applications or environments with limited space. Hybrid drones combine features of fixed-wing and rotary blade drones, offering versatility in both flight endurance and maneuverability.</p></p>
