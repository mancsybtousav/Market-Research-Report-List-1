<p><p>Corrugating board/cardboard market types include single corrugated, double corrugated, and triple corrugated boards. Single corrugated boards have one layer of fluting between two linerboards, offering basic strength and protection. Double corrugated boards have two layers of fluting, providing enhanced durability for heavier items. Triple corrugated boards have three layers of fluting, making them ideal for the most demanding packaging needs. In addition to these main types, there are other specialty corrugated boards available in the market to cater to specific requirements and applications.</p></p>
