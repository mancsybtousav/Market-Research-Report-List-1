<p><strong>The Electro Hydraulic Servo Valve for Aircraft Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Nozzle Flapper Valve</li><li>Jet Action Valve</li><li>Direct Drive Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electro Hydraulic Servo Valve for Aircraft Market consists of three main types: Nozzle Flapper Valve, Jet Action Valve, and Direct Drive Valve. Nozzle Flapper Valve uses a combination of nozzle and flapper to control fluid flow. Jet Action Valve utilizes a jet of fluid to create mechanical force for control. Direct Drive Valve directly translates electrical signals into mechanical movements without the need for hydraulic amplification. These valves play a critical role in aircraft systems by precisely controlling fluid flow and ensuring accurate aircraft maneuvers.</p></p>
