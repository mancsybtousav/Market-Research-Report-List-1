<p><p>Soil stabilization equipment market can be categorized into different types such as reclaimer, mixers, rotors, spreader, and others. Reclaimer equipment is used to pulverize existing pavement materials for recycling. Mixers are used to blend stabilizing agents with soil. Rotors are used for mixing soil and stabilizers in place. Spreaders help in evenly distributing stabilizing agents on the soil surface. Other equipment includes graders, compactors, and soil injection systems for various soil stabilization applications.</p></p>
