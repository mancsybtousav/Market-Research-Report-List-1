<p><p>Nanowires are categorized into different types based on their composition and properties. Metal nanowires are made of metallic elements and are known for their high conductivity. Semiconductor nanowires are made of materials with semiconducting properties, making them suitable for electronic applications. Oxide nanowires consist of metal oxide materials and are commonly used in sensors and catalysts. Other types of nanowires include carbon nanotubes and polymer nanowires, each with unique applications and characteristics in various industries.</p></p>
