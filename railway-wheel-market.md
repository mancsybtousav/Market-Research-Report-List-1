<p><p>The railway wheel market is divided into two main types: Cast Steel Wheel and Wrought Steel Wheel. Cast Steel Wheels are manufactured by pouring molten steel into a mold, resulting in a strong and durable wheel that is suitable for heavy-duty applications. On the other hand, Wrought Steel Wheels are crafted by forging and shaping solid steel into the desired shape, offering excellent impact resistance and stability. Both types of wheels play a crucial role in the efficient functioning of railway systems.</p></p>
