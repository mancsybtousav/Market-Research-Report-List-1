<p><p>Cold rolling oils and lubricants are used in the metalworking industry to reduce friction and heat during the cold rolling process. There are three main types of cold rolling oils/lubricants in the market: synthetic, mineral-based, and semi-synthetic. Synthetic oils are made from chemical compounds, offering high performance and durability. Mineral-based oils are derived from petroleum, providing good lubrication properties at a lower cost. Semi-synthetic oils are a blend of synthetic and mineral-based components, offering a balance between performance and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
