<p><p>The Cinema Visual and Audio Aids market includes two main types of devices: Assistive Listening Devices and Closed Captioning Devices. Assistive Listening Devices provide personalized audio amplification for individuals with hearing impairments, allowing them to better hear dialogue and sound effects in movies. Closed Captioning Devices display written text of dialogue and sound effects on a screen for individuals who are deaf or hard of hearing to follow along with the movie. Both types of devices improve accessibility and inclusivity for all moviegoers.</p></p>
