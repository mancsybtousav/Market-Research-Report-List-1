<p><p>Lithium-ion power battery material market is segmented into cathode material, anode material, electrolyte, and separator. Cathode materials are responsible for storing and releasing lithium ions during charge and discharge. Anode materials are able to store lithium ions when a battery is charged. Electrolytes conduct ions between cathode and anode. Separators prevent short circuits between cathode and anode while allowing ions to pass through. These components play crucial roles in the performance and lifespan of lithium-ion batteries.</p></p>
