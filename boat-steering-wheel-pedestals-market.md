<p><p>Boat steering wheel pedestals come in various types, including Pinion, Chain, Rack-and-pinion, and Others. Pinion systems utilize a gear mechanism for steering control, while Chain systems use a chain to transmit steering movements. Rack-and-pinion systems involve a gear set on a rack for precise steering. Other types of pedestals may include hydraulic steering systems or custom-built mechanisms. Each type offers different benefits in terms of responsiveness, ease of control, and durability, catering to the diverse needs of boat owners.</p></p>
