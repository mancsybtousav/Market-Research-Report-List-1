<p><p>RF Components (RFC) market consists of various types of components including Filters, Amplifiers, and Duplexers. Filters are used to allow specific frequencies to pass through while blocking others, Amplifiers are used to strengthen signals for transmission or reception, and Duplexers are used to separate incoming and outgoing signals in communication systems. These components are crucial in ensuring efficient and effective RF communication systems across various industries such as telecommunications, automotive, and aerospace.</p></p>
