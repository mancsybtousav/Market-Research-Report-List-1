<p><strong>The Tire Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Elastomers</li><li>Reinforcing Fillers</li><li>Plasticizers</li><li>Chemicals</li><li>Metal Reinforcements</li><li>Textile Reinforcements</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tire material market can be categorized into different types including elastomers, reinforcing fillers, plasticizers, chemicals, metal reinforcements, and textile reinforcements. Elastomers provide flexibility and resilience to the tire. Reinforcing fillers such as carbon black enhance the strength and durability of the tire. Plasticizers are used to improve the flexibility of the rubber. Chemicals are added for various purposes such as improving adhesion and reducing heat buildup. Metal reinforcements like steel belts provide structure and stability to the tire. Textile reinforcements help improve the overall strength and performance of the tire.</p></p>
