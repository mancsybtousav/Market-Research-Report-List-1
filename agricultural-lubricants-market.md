<p><p>Agricultural lubricants can be classified into three main types: mineral lubricants, synthetic lubricants, and bio-based lubricants. Mineral lubricants are derived from crude oil and offer good lubricating properties. Synthetic lubricants are manufactured using chemicals and provide enhanced performance and durability. Bio-based lubricants are made from renewable resources such as vegetable oils and offer environmentally friendly options. Each type of lubricant has its own unique characteristics and benefits, catering to different needs in the agricultural sector.</p></p>
