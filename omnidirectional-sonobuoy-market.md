<p><p>Omnidirectional Sonobuoys can be categorized into two main types: Wired Sonobuoys and Wireless Sonobuoys. Wired Sonobuoys are connected to the monitoring system using physical cables, providing a stable and secure connection but limiting the range of deployment. On the other hand, Wireless Sonobuoys utilize wireless communication technology to transmit data, offering greater flexibility in deployment and monitoring but may be susceptible to interference and security risks. Both types of Sonobuoys play a crucial role in underwater surveillance and detection operations.</p></p>
