<p><p>Acoustic-electric guitars come in two main types of pickups: electromagnetic and piezo. Electromagnetic pickups use a magnetic field to convert string vibrations into electrical signals, providing a warm, smooth tone popular in genres like jazz and blues. Piezo pickups, on the other hand, use crystals to convert vibrations into electrical signals, producing a clear, balanced sound ideal for genres like folk and country. Both types cater to different playing styles and preferences, offering musicians a diverse range of tonal options.</p></p>
