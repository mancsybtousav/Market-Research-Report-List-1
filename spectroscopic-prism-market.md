<p><p>Spectroscopic Prism market is categorized into four main types based on their functions. Dispersion Prism is used to disperse light into its spectral components. Deviation Prism is used to deviate light at a specified angle. Rotating Prism allows for adjustment of the angle of deviation. Total Reflection Prism is designed to reflect light internally at a high angle of incidence. Each type of prism serves a unique purpose in spectroscopic applications, catering to different requirements in various industries.</p></p>
