<p><p>Low-VOC coating additives market consists of various types such as rheology modifiers, defoamers, dispersants, wetting agents, and other additives. Rheology modifiers help in improving the flow and texture of the coating, while defoamers reduce foam formation during application. Dispersants aid in the dispersion of pigments and fillers, while wetting agents improve the wetting of the substrate. Other additives offer additional benefits such as improved adhesion, UV protection, or anti-corrosion properties. Each type plays a vital role in enhancing the performance and quality of low-VOC coatings.</p></p>
