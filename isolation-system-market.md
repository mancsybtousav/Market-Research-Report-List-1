<p><p>Isolation systems are designed to reduce the transmission of shock, vibration, or noise between connected structures. The three main types of isolation systems are shock absorbers, which absorb and dissipate the energy from sudden impacts; dampers, which reduce oscillations and vibrations in a system; and vibration isolators, which mitigate vibrations and shocks between two interconnected components. These systems are crucial in various industries such as aerospace, automotive, and construction to ensure smooth operation and reduce wear and tear on equipment.</p></p>
