<p><strong>The Phosphorus Starter Fertilizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ortho-phosphate</li><li>Poly-phosphate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Phosphorus starter fertilizers are categorized into two main types: Ortho-phosphate and Poly-phosphate. Ortho-phosphate fertilizers contain phosphorus in a form that is readily available for plants to absorb and utilize. These fertilizers are commonly used in field crops and help promote early plant growth. On the other hand, Poly-phosphate fertilizers release phosphorus slowly over time, providing a more sustained supply of nutrients to the plants. This type of fertilizer is often used in greenhouse settings or for crops with longer growing seasons.</p></p>
