<p><p>Laundry cleaning detergents come in four main types: anionic, cationic, non-ionic, and zwitterionic detergents. Anionic detergents are effective at removing dirt and grease due to their negative charge. Cationic detergents are great for disinfection and odor removal with their positive charge. Non-ionic detergents are gentle on fabrics and are often used in sensitive skin products. Zwitterionic detergents are versatile and can work in hard water. Each type of detergent offers unique benefits for different cleaning needs in the laundry market.</p></p>
