<p><strong>The Offshore Supply Vessel (OSV) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Anchor Handling Tug Supply</li><li>Platform Supply Vessel</li><li>Multipurpose Support Vessel</li><li>Standby & Rescue Vessel</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Offshore Supply Vessels (OSVs) come in different types to cater to various needs in the offshore industry. Anchor Handling Tug Supply vessels are equipped for towing rigs and structures to location and anchor handling operations. Platform Supply Vessels transport supplies to offshore platforms. Multipurpose Support Vessels perform a range of tasks including construction support and maintenance. Standby & Rescue Vessels provide emergency response services. Other types of OSVs include seismic support vessels and crew transfer vessels among others, each serving unique functions in the offshore sector.</p></p>
