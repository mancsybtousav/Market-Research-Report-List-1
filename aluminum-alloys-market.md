<p><p>Aluminum Alloys Market consists of two main types: Wrought Alloys and Cast Alloys. Wrought Alloys are produced through processes like rolling, forging, or extrusion, making them more malleable and capable of being shaped into various forms. They are commonly used in applications requiring high strength and durability, such as aerospace components and automotive parts. On the other hand, Cast Alloys are made by pouring molten aluminum into molds and are characterized by their excellent fluidity and casting properties, making them suitable for applications like automotive engine components and household appliances.</p></p>
