<p><p>Hand sanitizer lotion market consists of two main types: alcohol-based and alcohol-free. Alcohol-based hand sanitizers contain around 60-95% alcohol, such as ethanol or isopropyl alcohol, which effectively kills germs and bacteria. On the other hand, alcohol-free hand sanitizers rely on other active ingredients like benzalkonium chloride or triclosan to provide antimicrobial protection. While alcohol-based sanitizers are preferred for their fast-drying and germ-killing properties, alcohol-free sanitizers are popular among individuals with sensitive skin or those looking for a gentler alternative.</p></p>
