<p><strong>The Wheeled Stretcher Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Manual Type</li><li>Electric Type</li><li>Hydraulic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wheeled stretcher market is categorized into three types: manual, electric, and hydraulic. Manual stretchers require human effort to mobilize patients, making them cost-effective and suitable for shorter distances. Electric stretchers are powered by electricity and involve minimal physical exertion, offering convenience and ease of use. Hydraulic stretchers utilize hydraulic systems to lift and lower patients effortlessly. They are versatile, efficient, and provide precise control over movements. Healthcare providers can choose the stretcher type that best suits their needs based on factors such as distance, patient load, and desired level of automation.</p></p>
