<p><strong>The High Pressure Galvanized Pipe Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Dip Galvanized Pipe</li><li>Cold Galvanized Pipe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The High Pressure Galvanized Pipe market consists of two main types: Hot Dip Galvanized Pipe and Cold Galvanized Pipe. Hot Dip Galvanized Pipe involves immersing the pipe in a molten zinc bath to coat it and provide excellent corrosion resistance. Cold Galvanized Pipe, on the other hand, uses an electrochemical process to apply a thin layer of zinc onto the surface of the pipe. Both types are widely used in high pressure applications due to their durability and resistance to rust and corrosion.</p></p>
