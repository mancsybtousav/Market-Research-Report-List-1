<p><ul><li>Blow</li><li>Suction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automobile Seat Ventilation System market consists of two main types: Blow and Suction systems. Blow systems work by blowing cool or warm air through the seat's perforations to provide a comfortable temperature for the occupant. On the other hand, Suction systems work by sucking out the warm or hot air from the seat to maintain a cooler temperature. Both systems aim to enhance the overall comfort and experience for the passengers by regulating the seat temperature according to their preference.</p></p>
