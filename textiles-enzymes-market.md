<p><p>Textiles enzymes are used in various processes like desizing, scouring, bleaching, dyeing, and finishing in the textile industry to enhance the quality of fabrics and reduce energy and water consumption. The main types of textiles enzymes include Cellulase, Amylase, Catalase, Pectinase, Laccase, and others. Cellulase enzymes break down cellulose in fabrics, Amylase enzymes remove starch, Catalase enzymes eliminate hydrogen peroxide, Pectinase enzymes reduce pectin content, and Laccase enzymes play a role in dyeing processes. These enzymes help in making the textile industry more sustainable and efficient.</p></p>
