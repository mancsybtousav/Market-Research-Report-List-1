<p><ul><li>Photomask CD-SEM</li><li>Wafer CD-SEM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Semiconductor CD-SEM market can be broadly categorized into Photomask CD-SEM and Wafer CD-SEM markets. Photomask CD-SEMs are used for measuring critical dimensions on photomasks, which are essential in the lithography process for creating semiconductor devices. On the other hand, Wafer CD-SEMs are used for measuring critical dimensions directly on wafers during the semiconductor manufacturing process. Both types of CD-SEMs play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and precision of semiconductor devices.</p></p>
