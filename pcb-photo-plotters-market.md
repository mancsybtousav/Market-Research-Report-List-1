<p><p>PCB photo plotters are used in the manufacturing of printed circuit boards. External drum laser photoplotters are designed with the imaging drum located outside the machine, allowing for faster plotting speeds and higher precision. Internal drum laser photoplotters have the imaging drum located inside the machine, which can result in more accurate and consistent plotting, but may have slightly slower speeds. Both types of photoplotters are essential in the production of high-quality PCBs with precise circuitry designs.</p></p>
