<p><p>Metallized plastic film capacitors are categorized into two main types: AC film capacitors and DC film capacitors. AC film capacitors are designed for use in alternating current circuits, offering high levels of reliability, low loss, and endurance against voltage fluctuations. On the other hand, DC film capacitors are specifically built for direct current applications, providing stable capacitance and high insulation levels. Both types of capacitors are essential components in various electronic devices and systems, contributing to their efficient performance and functionality.</p></p>
