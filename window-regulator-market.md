<p><p>The window regulator market comprises two main types: electric window regulators and manual window regulators. Electric window regulators are operated by an electric motor, providing convenience and ease of use for adjusting car windows. On the other hand, manual window regulators rely on manual hand cranking for window adjustment. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, with electric window regulators being more popular in modern vehicles for their automated functionality, while manual window regulators are seen in some older models or budget-friendly options.</p></p>
