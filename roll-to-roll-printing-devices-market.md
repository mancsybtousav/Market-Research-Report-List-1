Gravure printing offers high-quality images and is commonly used for packaging and label printing. Offset lithography is suitable for printing on various materials. Flexography is ideal for printing on flexible materials like packaging and labels. Inkjet printing is versatile and cost-effective. Rotary screen printing is popular for textiles and other applications. Other specialized printing technologies cater to specific needs in the market.</p></p>
