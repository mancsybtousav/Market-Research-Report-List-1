<p><p>The low-frequency speaker market consists of various types of speakers designed to reproduce different ranges of bass frequencies. Woofers are larger speakers that handle low-frequency ranges, providing deep and powerful bass. Midwoofers are slightly smaller and focus on mid-bass frequencies for more balanced sound. Subwoofers are dedicated to handling the lowest frequencies, delivering intense and rumbling bass. Rotary subwoofers use a unique design that produces bass by rotating a fan-like blade. Each type of speaker serves a specific purpose in enhancing the overall sound quality of audio systems.</p></p>
