<p><p>Copper alloy strips encompass a variety of types including Bronze Strip, Brass Strip, and Cupronickel Strip. Bronze Strip is a versatile alloy made from copper and tin, known for its strength and corrosion resistance. Brass Strip is created from a mixture of copper and zinc, offering a balance of strength and malleability. Cupronickel Strip is made from a combination of copper and nickel, valued for its high resistance to corrosion in seawater. These alloy strips are commonly used in various industries for their unique properties and applications.</p></p>
