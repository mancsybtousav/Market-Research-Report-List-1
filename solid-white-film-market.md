<p><p>Solid white film is available in two main market types: Heat Sealable Solid White Film and Non-heat Sealable Solid White Film. Heat sealable films are designed to bond together with heat during packaging, providing a secure seal. These films are commonly used in industries such as food packaging. Non-heat sealable films do not require heat for sealing and are often used in applications where heat sealing is not practical or necessary. Both types offer a variety of benefits and are widely used in packaging and other industries.</p></p>
