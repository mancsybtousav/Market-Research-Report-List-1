<p><p>Dental laboratory sealers refer to various types of equipment used to seal dental materials during the fabrication process. Laser sealers utilize laser technology to create a tight seal. Infrared sealers utilize infrared heat to melt and bond the materials. Pulse ARC sealers use an electrical discharge to create the seal. Rotary sealers involve the use of a rotating element to create the seal. Other types of sealers may include ultrasonic or heat sealers. Each type of sealer offers unique advantages and is chosen based on specific requirements in the dental laboratory.</p></p>
