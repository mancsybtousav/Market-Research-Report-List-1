<p><p>The electronic scoreboard market offers two primary types: wall-mounted and desktop. Wall-mounted scoreboards are typically larger in size and designed to be mounted on walls or poles for high visibility in sports arenas and stadiums. On the other hand, desktop scoreboards are smaller and more portable, often used in smaller sports events, classrooms, or offices. Both types of scoreboards feature LED displays for showing scores, player statistics, and game time, providing real-time information to players, coaches, and spectators.</p></p>
