<p><p>The Spring Failsafe Brake market consists of two main types - the Dualspring Brake and the Monospring Brake. The Dualspring Brake utilizes two separate springs to ensure brake operation in case of failure, providing an extra layer of safety. On the other hand, the Monospring Brake utilizes a single spring mechanism to achieve the same failsafe function. Both types are designed to prevent accidents and ensure optimal brake performance in various applications.</p></p>
