<p><p>Ultrasonic metal welding systems are used to join metals together without the need for additional materials such as solder or adhesives. This process utilizes high-frequency ultrasonic vibrations to create friction heat at the interface of the metals, forming a solid-state bond. Ultrasonic metal welding systems offer advantages such as fast welding speeds, low energy consumption, and precise control over the welding process.</p><p>The ultrasonic metal welding systems market is expected to grow at a significant rate, with a projected CAGR of 11.6% during the forecast period.
