<p><p>Redundant power supplies market offers various configurations to enhance reliability and prevent system downtime. In a 1+1 power configuration, there are two power supplies where one acts as a backup. The 2+1 power configuration includes two active power supplies and one backup. The 2+2 power configuration consists of two active power supplies and two backup ones for added redundancy. Each configuration provides a different level of protection against power failures, catering to the diverse needs of different industries and applications.</p></p>
