<p><p>Polyisoprene latex is divided into two major market types - industrial grade and medical grade. Industrial grade polyisoprene latex is primarily used in the manufacturing of various products such as adhesives, sealants, and coatings due to its high tensile strength and excellent tear resistance. On the other hand, medical grade polyisoprene latex is specifically formulated for use in medical devices like surgical gloves and condoms, as it offers superior biocompatibility and hypoallergenic properties, making it safe for direct skin contact.</p></p>
