<p><p>The adhesive market can be categorized into three main types based on their composition and application methods. Water-based adhesives are environmentally friendly, easy to clean, and widely used in various industries. Solvent-based adhesives are known for their strong bonding properties and versatility in different applications. Hot melt adhesives are solid at room temperature but liquify when heated, providing fast and strong bonding capabilities. Each type of adhesive caters to different needs and preferences in the market, offering a wide range of options for various industries.</p></p>
