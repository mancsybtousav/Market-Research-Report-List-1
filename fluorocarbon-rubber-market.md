VDF offers good weather resistance, HFP provides flexibility, TFE brings chemical resistance, and PMVE offers improved adhesion properties. These different types cater to various industrial applications such as seals, gaskets, O-rings, and hoses where resistance to extreme temperatures, chemicals, and oils is required. The diversity in market types allows for customization based on specific performance requirements.</p></p>
