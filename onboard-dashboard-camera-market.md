<p><p>The onboard dashboard camera market consists of two main types: single-channel and multi-channel systems. Single-channel cameras typically record footage from one perspective, usually facing the front of a vehicle. Multi-channel cameras, on the other hand, can capture footage from multiple angles simultaneously, providing a more comprehensive view of the vehicle's surroundings. While single-channel cameras are simpler and more affordable, multi-channel cameras offer a greater level of security and protection for drivers.</p></p>
