<p><p>Nickel Manganese Cobalt (NMC) batteries come in different compositions, known as NMC111, NMC532, and NMC442. Each type refers to the ratio of nickel, manganese, and cobalt in the battery cathode material. NMC111 contains equal parts of nickel, manganese, and cobalt, while NMC532 contains more nickel and less cobalt. NMC442 has a higher manganese content. These different compositions offer varying levels of energy density, stability, and cost, making them suitable for different applications in the energy storage market.</p></p>
