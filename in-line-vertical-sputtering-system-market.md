<p><strong>The In-line Vertical Sputtering System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>In-line Vertical Static Sputtering System</li><li>In-line Vertical Dynamic Sputtering System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In-line vertical sputtering systems are commonly categorized into two main types: In-line Vertical Static Sputtering System and In-line Vertical Dynamic Sputtering System. In-line Vertical Static Sputtering System refers to a sputtering process where the substrate remains stationary during the coating process. On the other hand, In-line Vertical Dynamic Sputtering System involves continuous movement of the substrate during coating. Both systems are widely used in the manufacturing industry for depositing thin films onto various materials for applications such as electronics, optics, and solar panels.</p></p>
