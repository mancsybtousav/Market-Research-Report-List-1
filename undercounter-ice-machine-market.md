<p><ul><li>Air-Cooled Ice Machines</li><li>Water-Cooled Ice Machines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Undercounter ice machines are available in two main types: air-cooled and water-cooled. Air-cooled ice machines use air to cool the condenser coils and release heat, making them more energy efficient and cheaper to operate. Water-cooled ice machines, on the other hand, use water to cool the condenser coils, making them ideal for areas with limited ventilation but consuming more water and being more expensive to operate. Customers can choose between these options based on their specific needs and budget.</p></p>
