<p><p>Variable frequency servo drives are categorized based on the voltage they operate at, with two main types being low voltage and medium voltage drives. Low voltage drives typically operate at voltages below 1000V and are commonly used in smaller applications such as residential and commercial buildings. Medium voltage drives, on the other hand, operate at voltages above 1000V and are often used in industrial applications such as mining, oil and gas, and water treatment plants. Both types of drives offer precise control over motor speed and torque, enhancing overall performance and efficiency.</p></p>
