<p><p>Sulphur dyes are primarily categorized into two types: powdered sulphur dyes and solubilized sulphur dyes. Powdered sulphur dyes are insoluble in water and are typically used for dyeing cotton and other cellulosic fibers. On the other hand, solubilized sulphur dyes are water-soluble and are suitable for dyeing polyester and other synthetic fibers. Both types of sulphur dyes are known for their excellent color fastness and are widely used in the textile industry for dyeing various fabrics.</p></p>
