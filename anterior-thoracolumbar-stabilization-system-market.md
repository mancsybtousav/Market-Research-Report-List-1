<p><p>Anterior Thoracolumbar Stabilization System includes Monoaxial and Polyaxial Systems. Monoaxial systems have screws that can only be inserted along a single axis, providing stability in a straight line. Polyaxial systems have screws that can be inserted in multiple directions, allowing for greater flexibility and ease of insertion in complex anatomical locations. Both systems are used for stabilizing the thoracolumbar spine during spinal surgeries, with each offering unique advantages based on the specific needs of the patient.</p></p>
