<p><p>Thin films photovoltaic market can be categorized into two main types: organic photovoltaic and inorganic photovoltaic. Organic photovoltaic cells use organic materials such as polymers or small molecules to convert sunlight into electricity. These cells are more flexible and lightweight compared to inorganic photovoltaic cells. Inorganic photovoltaic cells, on the other hand, are made of materials like silicon or cadmium telluride and are known for their high efficiency and durability. Both types of cells play a crucial role in the development of renewable energy technologies.</p></p>
