<p><ul><li>Capacitance Thermal Dilatometer</li><li>Connecting Rod Thermal Dilatometer</li><li>Optical Thermal Dilatometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Portable Thermal Dilatometers are commonly categorized into three main types: Capacitance Thermal Dilatometers, Connecting Rod Thermal Dilatometers, and Optical Thermal Dilatometers. Capacitance Thermal Dilatometers measure the change in capacitance of a material as it expands or contracts with temperature. Connecting Rod Thermal Dilatometers use a connecting rod to measure the expansion or contraction of a material. Optical Thermal Dilatometers utilize optical techniques to measure changes in dimensions of a sample with temperature. Each type offers unique advantages and applications in various industries.</p></p>
