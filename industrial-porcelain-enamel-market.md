<p><ul><li>Powder</li><li>Liquid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Porcelain Enamel market consists of two main types: Powder and Liquid enamels. Powder enamels are dry powders that are melted onto a metal surface to create a durable and smooth finish. Liquid enamels, on the other hand, are liquid coatings that are sprayed or dipped onto the metal surface before being fired at high temperatures to create a hard, glossy finish. Both types of enamels offer excellent corrosion resistance, heat resistance, and durability, making them popular choices for various industrial applications.</p></p>
