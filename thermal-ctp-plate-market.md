<p><p>Thermal CTP Plate market includes three main types of plates: Positive Thermal CTP Plate, which is exposed to heat to form images; Negative Thermal CTP Plate, which is sensitive to heat and becomes non-image areas when exposed; and UV CTP Plate, which uses ultraviolet light to form images. Each type of plate offers different advantages depending on the specific needs of the printing process, such as faster processing times, better resolution, and longer run lengths.</p></p>
