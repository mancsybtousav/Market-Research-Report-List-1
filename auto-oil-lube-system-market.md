<p><strong>The Auto Oil Lube System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct System</li><li>Indirect System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Auto Oil Lube System market is divided into two main types: Direct System and Indirect System. In a Direct System, the oil is directly pumped to the engine components that need lubrication, providing more precise and efficient lubrication. On the other hand, an Indirect System circulates the oil through a filter before distributing it to the engine, offering better contamination control. Both types have their own advantages and are suitable for different types of vehicles and applications.</p></p>
