<p><p>Industrial coated fabrics market consists of polymer coated fabrics, rubber coated fabrics, and fabric backed wall coverings. Polymer coated fabrics are made by coating a layer of polymer onto a fabric substrate, providing high durability and resistance to water. Rubber coated fabrics are created by adding a rubber coating to fabrics, offering flexibility and abrasion resistance. Fabric backed wall coverings are fabrics laminated with a backing material, used for decorative and protective purposes in interior design. Each type offers unique properties and applications in various industries.</p></p>
