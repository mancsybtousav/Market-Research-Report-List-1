<p><ul><li>Polymer Matrix Composite Materials</li><li>Ceramic Matrix Composite Materials</li><li>Metal Matrix Composite Materials</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High-temperature composite materials are divided into three main types based on the matrix material used: polymer matrix composite materials, ceramic matrix composite materials, and metal matrix composite materials. Polymer matrix composites consist of a polymer resin reinforced with fibers, providing lightweight and corrosion-resistant properties. Ceramic matrix composites use ceramic fibers in a ceramic matrix, offering high-temperature resistance and strength. Metal matrix composites combine metal with ceramic or carbon fibers to achieve high strength and stiffness at elevated temperatures, often used in aerospace and automotive applications.</p></p>
