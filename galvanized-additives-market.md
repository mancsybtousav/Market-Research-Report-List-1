<p><p>Galvanized additives market consists of various types of additives that are essential for the galvanizing process. Degreasing agents are used to remove grease and oil from the surface, while cleaning additives help in removing dirt and impurities. Inhibitors are added to prevent unwanted reactions during the galvanizing process. Pickling additives help in removing impurities and creating a clean surface for galvanizing. Scaling powder is used for descaling the surface. Passivators are used to enhance the corrosion resistance of the galvanized coating. Other additives may include fluxes and brighteners.</p></p>
