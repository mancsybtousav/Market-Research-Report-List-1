<p><p>The Non-Thermal Pasteurization Equipment market includes various types such as High Pressure Processing (HPP), Pulse Electric Field (PEF), Microwave Volumetric Heating (MVH), Irradiation, Ultrasonic, and others. HPP involves applying high pressure to kill bacteria, while PEF uses electrical pulses. MVH heats food using microwaves without changing its taste or texture. Irradiation uses ionizing radiation to sterilize food. Ultrasonic technology utilizes sound waves to kill pathogens. Other methods may include UV light or ozone treatment for food preservation.</p></p>
