<p><p>Complex Programmable Logic Devices (CPLDs) are available in two main types - Surface-Mount Technology (SMT/SMD) CPLDs and Through-Hole CPLDs. SMT/SMD CPLDs are mounted directly onto the surface of a printed circuit board, making them more compact and suitable for high-density applications. In contrast, Through-Hole CPLDs are mounted through holes on the PCB, offering better mechanical stability and suitability for harsh environments. Both types of CPLDs are widely used in various industries for logic functions, signal processing, and control applications.</p></p>
