<p><strong>The Electronic Compact Fan Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Fan</li><li>Axial Fan</li><li>Mixed Flow Fan</li><li>Cross Flow Fan</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electronic compact fan market includes various types such as centrifugal fans, axial fans, mixed flow fans, and cross flow fans. Centrifugal fans are designed to move air radially outward, while axial fans move air parallel to the fan axis. Mixed flow fans combine features of both centrifugal and axial fans to provide a more balanced airflow. Cross flow fans move air perpendicular to the fan axis, making them suitable for applications where space is limited. Each type of fan has its own unique characteristics and benefits, catering to different needs in the electronic compact fan market.</p></p>
