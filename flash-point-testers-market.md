<p><ul><li>Open Cup Flash Point Tester</li><li>Closed Cup Flash Point Tester</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flash Point Testers are classified into two types based on the design of the apparatus used for testing: Open Cup Flash Point Testers and Closed Cup Flash Point Testers. Open Cup testers have the sample exposed to the air during testing, allowing for quicker results but may be less accurate due to air currents. Closed Cup testers have the sample enclosed during testing, providing more accurate results but taking longer to reach equilibrium. Both types of testers are used in various industries to determine the flash point of flammable liquids.</p></p>
