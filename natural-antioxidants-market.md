Vitamin E acts as a fat-soluble antioxidant that plays a vital role in protecting cell membranes. Carotenoids are pigments found in plants that have antioxidant properties. Polyphenols are a group of compounds found in plants that have antioxidant effects and may help reduce the risk of chronic diseases.</p></p>
