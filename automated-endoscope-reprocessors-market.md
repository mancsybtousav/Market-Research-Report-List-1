<p><p>Automated endoscope reprocessors, also known as AERs, are machines used in medical settings to disinfect and clean endoscopes. There are two main types of AERs: single chamber and multi chamber. </p><p>Single chamber AERs are equipped with a single chamber where the entire reprocessing cycle takes place. They offer efficient and automated cleaning and disinfection of endoscopes. </p><p>On the other hand, multi chamber AERs have multiple chambers that allow simultaneous reprocessing of several endoscopes. This enables healthcare facilities to process a higher volume of endoscopes in a shorter time frame. Both types of AERs play a crucial role in maintaining the safety and efficacy of endoscopy procedures.</p></p>
