<p><p>Air suspension solenoid valves are crucial components of air suspension systems, controlling the flow of compressed air to adjust the vehicle's ride height and stiffness. There are two main types of solenoid valves based on port configuration - three ports and six ports. Three port valves regulate the air flow between two chambers, while six port valves control air flow between four chambers, allowing for more precise control over the suspension system. Both types play a key role in maintaining a smooth and comfortable ride in vehicles equipped with air suspension.</p></p>
