<p><strong>The Oil Based Defoamer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mineral Oil Based</li><li>Polyether Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Oil based defoamers are divided into two main types - mineral oil based and polyether based. Mineral oil based defoamers are derived from petroleum and are known for their compatibility with a wide range of chemicals. They are effective in controlling foam in various industrial processes. Polyether based defoamers, on the other hand, are synthetic compounds that are especially effective in aqueous systems. They are known for their quick defoaming action and are commonly used in applications where mineral oil based defoamers may not be suitable.</p></p>
