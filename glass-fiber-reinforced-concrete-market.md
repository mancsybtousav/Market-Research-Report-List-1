<p><p>Glass Fiber Reinforced Concrete (GFRC) is categorized into different strength levels based on compressive strength. Under C30 refers to GFRC with a compressive strength below 30MPa, C30-C60 denotes a range between 30-60MPa, C60-C100 falls in the 60-100MPa range, and Above C100 indicates a compressive strength exceeding 100MPa. These classifications help in identifying the suitable applications for GFRC based on the required strength and durability, catering to a wide range of construction projects.</p></p>
