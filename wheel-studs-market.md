<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Wheel Studs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Screw-in Wheel Studs</li><li>Press-in Wheel Studs</li><li>Welded-in Wheel Studs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wheel studs are commonly categorized into three main types: screw-in wheel studs, press-in wheel studs, and welded-in wheel studs. Screw-in wheel studs are designed to be screwed into the hub of the wheel, providing a secure connection. Press-in wheel studs are pressed into the hub, creating a tight fit. Welded-in wheel studs are welded directly onto the hub, ensuring a strong and durable connection. Each type of wheel stud offers different advantages and is chosen based on the specific needs of the vehicle or application.</p></p>
