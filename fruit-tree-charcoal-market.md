<p><ul><li>Lump Charcoal</li><li>Sugar Charcoal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fruit tree charcoal refers to charcoal made from fruit wood. There are two main types of fruit tree charcoal markets: lump charcoal and sugar charcoal. Lump charcoal is made by burning wood in a low-oxygen environment, resulting in pure carbon chunks. Sugar charcoal, on the other hand, is produced by carbonizing the sugary sap of fruit trees. Both types of charcoal are popular among BBQ enthusiasts for their clean burning properties and ability to impart unique flavors to grilled foods.</p></p>
