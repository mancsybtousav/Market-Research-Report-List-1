<p><p>Earth Leakage Circuit Breakers are safety devices that quickly disconnect electrical circuits when they detect any imbalance between the incoming and outgoing currents, which could indicate a potential leakage to the ground. There are two main types on the market: Voltage Earth Leakage Circuit Breakers (VELCB) which operate based on the voltage difference, and Current Earth Leakage Circuit Breakers (CELCB) which operate based on the current difference. Both types are crucial for protecting against electric shocks and preventing electrical fires in residential and industrial settings.</p></p>
