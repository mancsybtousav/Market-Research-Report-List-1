<p><p>Radiation shielding walls are designed to protect individuals from harmful radiation exposure in medical facilities, laboratories, and industrial settings. Lead lined sheetrock is a common type of shielding wall made by embedding lead sheets within gypsum boards. Lead plywood is another option, with lead sheets sandwiched between layers of plywood for added strength. Lead bricks are also used for radiation shielding walls, offering a more versatile and customizable solution. These different types of shielding walls provide varying levels of protection depending on the specific needs of the environment.</p></p>
