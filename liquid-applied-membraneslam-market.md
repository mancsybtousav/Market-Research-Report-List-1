<p><p>Liquid Applied Membranes (LAM) market types include Bituminous, Elastomeric, Cementitious, and Others. Bituminous membranes are made from asphalt or coal tar pitch and are ideal for waterproofing. Elastomeric membranes are flexible, durable, and resistant to UV rays, making them suitable for outdoor applications. Cementitious membranes consist of cement and additives, providing high performance and chemical resistance. Other types of LAM may include polyurethane, polyurea, and acrylic membranes, offering various features and benefits for different construction needs.</p></p>
