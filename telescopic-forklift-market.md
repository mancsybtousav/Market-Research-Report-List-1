<p><p>A telescopic forklift, also known as a telehandler, is a versatile piece of material handling equipment that combines the functionality of a crane and a forklift. Telescopic forklifts are commonly used in construction, agriculture, landscaping, and other industries that require lifting and moving heavy loads over rough terrain.</p><p>The Telescopic Forklift Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12% during the forecast period. Overall, the telescopic forklift market is expected to experience steady growth in the coming years.</p></p>
