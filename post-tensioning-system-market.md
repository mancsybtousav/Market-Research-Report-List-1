<p><p>The Post-Tensioning System market consists of two main types: Unbonded Post-Tensioning System and Bonded Post-Tensioning System. Unbonded Post-Tensioning System involves installing individual tendons, which are then protected with a plastic sheath and greased to prevent corrosion. This type offers flexibility and ease of installation. Bonded Post-Tensioning System, on the other hand, comprises steel tendons encased in a duct and bonded with concrete. This system provides better load transfer and structural integrity. Both types find application in various construction projects, depending on the specific requirements and budget constraints.</p></p>
