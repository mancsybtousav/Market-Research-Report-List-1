<p><p>PVC Electrical Conduit Pipe Market consists of two main types of pipes - Rigid Conduit Pipes and Flexible Conduit Pipes. Rigid Conduit Pipes are typically used in applications where protection and durability are key, such as outdoor installations or areas prone to physical damage.
