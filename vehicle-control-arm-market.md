<p><ul><li>Stamped Steel Control Arms</li><li>Cast Iron Control Arms</li><li>Cast Aluminum Control Arms</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vehicle control arms are essential components that connect the suspension system to the vehicle frame. They come in three main types: stamped steel control arms, cast iron control arms, and cast aluminum control arms. Stamped steel control arms are cost-effective and commonly used in vehicles. Cast iron control arms are more durable and suitable for heavy-duty applications. Cast aluminum control arms are lightweight and offer better corrosion resistance. Each type has its own advantages and is chosen based on the vehicle's needs and requirements.</p></p>
