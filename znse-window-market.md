<p><ul><li>Uncoated</li><li>AR Coated</li><li>Other Coating Options</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ZnSe windows are available in three main market types: uncoated, AR coated, and other coating options. Uncoated ZnSe windows provide basic protection and have a high transmission rate. AR (anti-reflection) coated ZnSe windows reduce reflection and glare, improving optical performance. Other coating options, such as hydrophobic or oleophobic coatings, provide additional functionalities like increased durability or resistance to oils and liquids. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in various industries, such as aerospace, defense, and medical fields.</p></p>
