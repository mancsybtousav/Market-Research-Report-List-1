<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Cabinet Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-Based</li><li>Water-Based</li><li>Solventless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cabinet adhesives are categorized into three main types: solvent-based, water-based, and solventless. Solvent-based adhesives contain volatile organic compounds for bonding cabinets and furniture. Water-based adhesives are eco-friendly and contain water as the main solvent, making them safe to use. Solventless adhesives do not contain any solvents and are typically two-component products that require mixing before application. Each type has its advantages and disadvantages, depending on the specific requirements of the cabinet assembly process.</p></p>
