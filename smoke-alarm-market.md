<p><ul><li>Photoelectric Smoke Alarms</li><li>Ionization Smoke Alarms</li><li>Combination Smoke Alarms</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smoke alarm market consists of three types: photoelectric smoke alarms, ionization smoke alarms, and combination smoke alarms. Photoelectric smoke alarms are designed to detect larger smoke particles from smoldering fires, while ionization smoke alarms are more sensitive to smaller smoke particles from flaming fires. Combination smoke alarms incorporate both technologies to provide comprehensive protection. Each type of smoke alarm offers unique advantages in detecting different types of fires, making it important for consumers to choose the right type for their specific needs.</p></p>
