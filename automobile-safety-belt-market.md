<p><p>The Automobile Safety Belt market is mainly classified into two types - ELR Three Point Belt and ALR Three Point Belt. ELR stands for Emergency Locking Retractor, which automatically locks the seat belt during sudden stops or impact. ALR stands for Automatic Locking Retractor, which locks the seat belt in place once it is pulled out to a certain length. Both types offer enhanced safety features and are designed to protect passengers in the event of a collision or sudden braking.</p></p>
