<p><p>SDS-PAGE market consists of three main types: gels, reagents, and instruments. Gels are used as the separation medium in the electrophoresis process, allowing proteins to migrate based on their size. Reagents are chemicals used to prepare samples, run the gel, and visualize proteins. Instruments include electrophoresis systems and imaging equipment necessary for conducting SDS-PAGE. Together, these components form a comprehensive market catering to researchers and scientists in the fields of biochemistry, molecular biology, and biotechnology.</p></p>
