<p><p>Acids for skincare can be categorized into three main types: AHA (Alpha Hydroxy Acids), BHA (Beta Hydroxy Acids), and other acids. AHA acids, such as glycolic and lactic acid, are known for their exfoliating properties and ability to improve skin texture and tone. BHA acids, such as salicylic acid, are oil-soluble and are effective in treating acne and reducing inflammation. Other acids, such as hyaluronic acid and niacinamide, offer various skincare benefits like hydration and skin brightening.</p></p>
