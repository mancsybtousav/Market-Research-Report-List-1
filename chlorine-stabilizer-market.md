<p><p>Chlorine stabilizer, also known as cyanuric acid, is essential for maintaining the effectiveness of chlorine in pools. There are two main types of chlorine stabilizer products available - cyanuric acid tablets and cyanuric acid granular. Cyanuric acid tablets are slow-dissolving and commonly used in skimmers or floaters, while cyanuric acid granular is quick-dissolving and usually added directly to the pool. Both types help to prevent chlorine from being broken down by sunlight, extending the life of chlorine in the water.</p></p>
