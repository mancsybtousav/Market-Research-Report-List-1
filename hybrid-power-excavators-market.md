<p><p>Hydraulic-based Hybrid Excavators utilize a combination of diesel engine and hydraulic energy storage to operate, allowing for increased efficiency and reduced fuel consumption. Electric-based Hybrid Excavators, on the other hand, use a combination of electric motor and battery power for operation, resulting in lower emissions and quieter operation. Both types of hybrid power excavators offer environmentally friendly alternatives to traditional excavators while still providing the power and performance needed for various construction and excavation tasks.</p></p>
