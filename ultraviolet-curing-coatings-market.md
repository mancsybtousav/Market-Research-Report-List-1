<p><p>Ultraviolet curing coatings can be categorized into water-based and powder-based types. Water-based UV coatings are environmentally friendly and provide good adhesion to various substrates, making them suitable for applications in industries such as automotive, wood, and packaging. On the other hand, powder-based UV coatings offer high durability and chemical resistance, making them ideal for high-performance applications like aerospace and electronics. Both types of coatings are gaining popularity in the market due to their fast curing time and low volatile organic compound emissions.</p></p>
