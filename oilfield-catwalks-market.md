<p><ul><li>Mechanized</li><li>Hydraulic</li><li>Automated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Oilfield catwalks are used in drilling operations to safely transport tubular goods on and off drilling rigs. There are three main types of catwalks in the market: mechanized, hydraulic, and automated. Mechanized catwalks require manual operation for loading and unloading. Hydraulic catwalks use hydraulic power to move tubular goods. Automated catwalks are the most advanced, using sensors and robotics to fully automate the loading and unloading process. Each type offers different levels of efficiency and safety in oilfield operations.</p></p>
