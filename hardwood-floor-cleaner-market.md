<p><p>The hardwood floor cleaner market can be divided into three main types: water cleaner, semi-aqueous cleaner, and non-aqueous cleaner. Water cleaners are primarily made with water and are considered the most environmentally friendly option. Semi-aqueous cleaners contain a mixture of water and other solvents, offering a balance between effectiveness and eco-friendliness. Non-aqueous cleaners, on the other hand, do not contain any water and are typically more powerful but less environmentally friendly. Each type of cleaner appeals to different consumer preferences and cleaning needs.</p></p>
