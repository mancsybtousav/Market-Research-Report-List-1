Carboxylic acid type polyurethanes contain carboxylic acid groups, which provide excellent adhesion and water resistance properties. Sulfonic acid type polyurethanes, on the other hand, contain sulfonic acid groups that enhance the dispersibility and stability of the polyurethane emulsions. Both types find applications in various industries such as coatings, adhesives, and sealants.</p></p>
