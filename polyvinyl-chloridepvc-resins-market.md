<p><p>Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC) resins are categorized into three main types: Suspension PVC Resins, Emulsion PVC Resins, and Blended PVC Resins. Suspension PVC resins are produced through a suspension polymerization process and are commonly used in construction materials. Emulsion PVC resins are made through an emulsion polymerization process and are known for their excellent clarity and flexibility. Blended PVC resins are a combination of suspension and emulsion resins, offering a balance of properties for various applications in industries such as automotive, packaging, and healthcare.</p></p>
