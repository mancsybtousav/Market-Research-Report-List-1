<p><ul><li>Vacuum Deposition</li><li>Coating Metal Powder With Binders</li><li>Sputter Coating</li><li>Electroless Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal fibres market can be divided into four types: Vacuum Deposition, Coating Metal Powder With Binders, Sputter Coating, and Electroless Coating. Vacuum Deposition involves depositing metal vapor onto a substrate. Coating Metal Powder With Binders mixes metal powder with binders to create metal fibres. Sputter Coating utilizes a vacuum chamber to sputter metal atoms onto a substrate. Electroless Coating involves using a chemical reaction to deposit metal onto a surface without the need for an external power source.</p></p>
