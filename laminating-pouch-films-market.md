<p><p>Laminating pouch films are used to protect and preserve documents or photos by encasing them in a protective layer. There are two main types of laminating pouch films: heat-sealed film and cold pouch film. Heat-sealed film requires a heat laminating machine to seal the edges of the pouch, while cold pouch film can be sealed by pressure alone. Both types offer different levels of flexibility, durability, and finish, catering to a variety of laminating needs.</p></p>
