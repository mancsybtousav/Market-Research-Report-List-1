<p><p>Barrier CMP slurry is a crucial component in semiconductor manufacturing, used to polish and planarize wafers during the fabrication process. Copper barrier slurry is specifically designed for copper interconnects, ensuring uniformity and preventing dishing and erosion. Tantalum barrier slurry is used for tantalum-based materials, providing excellent barrier properties and achieving high planarity. Both types of barrier slurry are essential for advanced semiconductor devices, enabling precise and efficient wafer processing in the production of cutting-edge electronics.</p></p>
