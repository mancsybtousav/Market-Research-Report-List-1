<p><p>Liquid Crystal on Silicon Display (LCoS) technology is divided into two main market types - Ferroelectric LCoS and Nematic LCoS. Ferroelectric LCoS displays use ferroelectric liquid crystals which provide faster response times and higher image quality, making them ideal for applications requiring high speed and resolution. On the other hand, Nematic LCoS displays use nematic liquid crystals and are more commonly used in consumer electronics due to their cost-effectiveness and versatility in display applications. Both types of LCoS displays offer high image quality and performance for various markets.</p></p>
