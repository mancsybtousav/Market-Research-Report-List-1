<p><p>Technical textile fabrics market consists of three main types of products: fabric, unspun fiber, and yarn-type products. Fabric includes materials like woven, non-woven, and knitted textiles used for various applications such as automotive, medical, and construction. Unspun fibers are individual fibers that are used in products like filters and reinforcement materials. Yarn-type products are strands of fibers twisted or spun together for applications like clothing, ropes, and geotextiles. Each type serves a unique purpose in the technical textile industry.</p></p>
