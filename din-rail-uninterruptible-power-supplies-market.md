<p><p>DIN Rail Uninterruptible Power Supplies (UPS) are commonly divided into two types: DIN Rail DC UPS and DIN Rail AC UPS. DIN Rail DC UPS are designed to provide backup power during outages by converting and storing direct current (DC) from a connected power source. On the other hand, DIN Rail AC UPS operate by converting and storing alternating current (AC) power, ensuring uninterrupted electricity supply to critical devices. Both types of UPS are compact, easy to install on standard DIN rails, and widely used in industrial automation and control systems.</p></p>
