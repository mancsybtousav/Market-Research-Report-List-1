<p><strong>The Brass Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Casting-Rolling ( CR )</li><li>Extrusion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Brass tube market can be divided into three main types: Casting, Rolling (CR), and Extrusion. Casting involves molten brass being poured into molds to create tubes of various shapes and sizes. Rolling (CR) involves passing brass through a series of rollers to achieve the desired dimensions and surface finish. Extrusion involves forcing heated brass through a die to produce tubes of consistent cross-sectional shapes. Each method has its own advantages and is used based on specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
