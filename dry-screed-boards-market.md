<p><ul><li>Wood Fibre Backed</li><li>Mineral Fibre Backed</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dry screed boards are commonly categorized into three main types based on their backing material: wood fibre backed, mineral fibre backed, and others. Wood fibre backed boards are made from compressed wood fibres and offer good insulation properties. Mineral fibre backed boards are typically made from rock wool or glass wool and provide excellent fire resistance. The "others" category may include boards made from alternative materials such as foam or cork. Each type of dry screed board has unique properties and advantages, catering to different needs in the construction industry.</p></p>
