<p><p>Semi-Automatic Cartoning Machine Market Types - V-FFS and H-FFS are two main categories based on the type of packaging they produce. V-FFS machines are vertical form-fill-seal systems where the product is filled into a bag and sealed vertically. On the other hand, H-FFS machines are horizontal form-fill-seal systems that package products in a horizontal manner. Both types have their own advantages and are suited for different industries based on factors like product size, shape, and packaging requirements.</p></p>
