<p><p>Automotive Ultracapacitors are energy storage devices used in vehicles for quick energy storage and release. Electrostatic Double-Layer Capacitors are traditional ultracapacitors that store energy through the separation of charge in an electrolyte. Hybrid Capacitors combine the features of ultracapacitors and batteries, providing high energy density and power density. These types of ultracapacitors are popular in the automotive industry for applications such as regenerative braking and start-stop systems due to their ability to quickly store and release energy.</p></p>
