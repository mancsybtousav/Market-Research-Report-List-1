<p><p>Machine Room Less (MRL) Passenger Elevator market is segmented into two types - Traction Passenger Elevator and Hydraulic Passenger Elevator. Traction Passenger Elevator uses a system of ropes and counterweights to move the elevator car, making it suitable for taller buildings. On the other hand, Hydraulic Passenger Elevator operates using a hydraulic pump to control the movement of the elevator car, often chosen for low to mid-rise buildings. Both types eliminate the need for a separate machine room, saving space and construction costs for building owners.</p></p>
