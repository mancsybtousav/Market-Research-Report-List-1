<p><p>Processed cheese machines come in two main types: automatic and semi-automatic. Automatic machines require minimal human intervention, as they can carry out multiple tasks such as mixing, heating, and packaging cheese slices or blocks. On the other hand, semi-automatic machines require some manual input from operators, such as loading ingredients or adjusting settings. Semi-automatic machines are often preferred by smaller businesses that do not have high production volumes or require more flexibility in their cheese processing operations.</p></p>
