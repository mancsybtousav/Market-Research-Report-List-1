<p><p>Biopolyols are a type of polyols commonly used in the production of various polyurethane products. There are two main types of Biopolyol markets: Polyether Polyol and Polyester Polyol. Polyether Polyols are derived from petrochemicals and are known for their flexibility and water resistance, making them suitable for applications such as foam insulation. On the other hand, Polyester Polyols are derived from natural sources like vegetable oils and are valued for their durability and heat resistance, making them ideal for applications like coatings and adhesives.</p></p>
