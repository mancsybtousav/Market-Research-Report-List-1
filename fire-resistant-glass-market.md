<p><p>Fire-resistant glass is a type of glass that can withstand high temperatures and prevent the spread of fire. There are four main types of fire-resistant glass in the market. Laminated fire-resistant glass consists of multiple layers of glass with a plastic interlayer, providing enhanced protection and visibility. Tempered fire-resistant glass is treated with heat to increase its strength and resistance to fire. Wired fire-resistant glass has embedded metal wires to hold the glass fragments in place during a fire. Ceramic fire-resistant glass is made from specialized ceramic materials, offering exceptional fire resistance and insulation properties.</p></p>
